Travel Insurance for Flight Travel

At Sunshine Airlines, we want to ensure that your journey is as smooth and worry-free as possible. That’s why we offer flexible Travel Insurance options to protect you from unforeseen circumstances during your travels. Whether you’re looking for basic coverage, a more comprehensive plan, or protection against trip cancellations, we have you covered.

1. Basic Coverage

Our Basic Travel Insurance is designed to provide essential protection, offering peace of mind without breaking the bank. It covers common travel issues and ensures you’re not left unprotected during your trip.

Key Benefits

  • Coverage for emergency medical treatments or hospital visits during your trip, including necessary medications.
  • Compensation for baggage that is lost, stolen, or delayed for more than 12 hours, ensuring you can purchase essentials until your baggage is returned.
  • Reimbursement for meals, accommodations, or transport costs in case of long flight delays, allowing you to manage unexpected situations without stress.
  • Access to a dedicated hotline for emergencies, providing assistance when you need it the most.

Who Is It For? This plan is ideal for travelers who want affordable protection for basic travel risks and minimal coverage. Perfect for short trips and weekend getaways.

2. Comprehensive Coverage

For travelers who want all-around protection, our Comprehensive Travel Insurance offers extensive coverage for a wide range of travel-related issues. It’s perfect for international travelers or those embarking on long trips where more risks are involved.

Key Benefits

  • Full coverage for medical emergencies, including hospital stays, medications, and emergency evacuations, ensuring you receive the care you need, wherever you are.
  • Reimbursement for unused travel and accommodation costs if your trip is cut short due to illness, injury, or other covered reasons, safeguarding your financial investment.
  • Extended coverage for lost, stolen, or damaged baggage, including coverage for valuable items like electronics and jewelry.
  • 24/7 access to global assistance services for medical and legal emergencies, providing peace of mind knowing help is just a call away.
  • Compensation for additional costs incurred due to flight delays, cancellations, or missed connections, helping you cover unexpected expenses.
  • Coverage for legal liability in case you cause damage or injury to others during your trip, protecting you from unforeseen legal costs.

Who Is It For? This plan is ideal for those who want extensive coverage, including medical, personal liability, and travel disruption protection. It’s recommended for international travelers, families, and anyone seeking more security during their trip.

3. Cancellation Coverage

Our Cancellation Travel Insurance protects you financially in case you need to cancel your trip due to unforeseen circumstances. Whether it’s due to personal emergencies, medical reasons, or other covered events, this plan ensures you won’t lose your investment.

Key Benefits

  • Reimbursement of non-refundable travel costs if you need to cancel your flight for a covered reason (e.g., illness, injury, death of a family member, or other emergencies).
  • If something goes wrong before your trip starts (like a sudden illness or job loss), you can cancel your flight and receive reimbursement for the costs.
  • Coverage for cancellations due to unexpected events like natural disasters, travel bans, or sudden work obligations, giving you the flexibility you need.
  • Ability to cancel parts of your trip (like a hotel stay) while keeping others intact, allowing for more control over your plans.

Who Is It For? This plan is perfect for travelers who want to protect their investment and reduce financial risks in case of unexpected trip cancellations. It’s especially recommended for those booking in advance or during uncertain times.

Why Choose Sunshine Airlines Travel Insurance?

Key Benefits

  • Choose from a variety of coverage levels to suit your budget and travel needs, ensuring you find a plan that fits your lifestyle.
  • Our claims process is straightforward, ensuring you can get the assistance and reimbursement you need quickly without unnecessary complications.
  • Access to emergency support services at any time, from anywhere in the world, ensuring help is available when you need it most.
  • Customize your insurance package to include specific benefits that align with your travel plans and personal needs.
  • With Sunshine Airlines Travel Insurance, focus on creating wonderful travel memories, knowing you have the protection and support you need for a travel with Peace of mind.
