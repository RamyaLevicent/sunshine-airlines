Welcome to Our Lounge Access Page

What is Lounge Access?

Airport lounges are exclusive spaces at the airport where travelers can relax, work, or enjoy refreshments before their flights. They provide a serene environment away from the hustle and bustle of the terminal, allowing you to recharge before your journey.

Eligibility for Lounge Access

  • First Class and Business Class Passengers: All first and business class passengers of Sunshine Airlines enjoy complimentary access to our lounges.
  • Frequent Flyer Members: Gold and Platinum members of our frequent flyer program are entitled to complimentary lounge access, providing them with a premium experience.
  • Lounge Membership: Passengers can purchase an annual lounge membership for a fee, granting access to all Sunshine Airlines lounges regardless of ticket class.
  • Paid Access: Economy class passengers can buy a one-time access pass to the lounge for a nominal fee, allowing them to enjoy the same benefits.

Lounge Amenities

Our lounges provide a variety of amenities designed to enhance your travel experience:

  • Complimentary snacks and beverages, including soft drinks, coffee, and light meals.
  • Free Wi-Fi access to stay connected while you wait for your flight.
  • Comfortable seating with charging ports to keep your devices powered.
  • Workstations equipped with computers for business travelers.
  • Showers and relaxation areas for those looking to freshen up before their journey.
  • Entertainment options, including television and reading materials.

How to Access the Lounge

Accessing our lounges is simple and straightforward:

  1. Locate the Lounge: Check your airport’s lounge directory for the Sunshine Airlines lounge location.
  2. Present Your Boarding Pass: Show your boarding pass at the lounge entrance for verification.
  3. Membership Card (if applicable): If you’re accessing the lounge through a membership or frequent flyer program, present the relevant card.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Can I bring guests into the lounge? Yes, eligible passengers can bring one guest into the lounge, subject to availability.

Are children allowed in the lounge? Children are welcome in our lounges, and we have special facilities available for families.

What should I do if I encounter issues accessing the lounge? If you face any issues, please contact our customer service team or the lounge staff for assistance.
