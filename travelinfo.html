Travel Informations for a Smooth Journey

1. Baggage Allowance & Guidelines

Carry-On Baggage:

Each passenger is allowed one carry-on bag and one personal item (such as a briefcase, laptop bag, or purse). Carry-on baggage should not exceed 22 x 14 x 9 inches in size and must fit in the overhead bin.

Checked Baggage:

Each passenger is allowed one checked bag that does not exceed 50 lbs in weight or 62 linear inches in size. Additional fees apply for excess baggage.

2. Check-In & Boarding Process

Online Check-In:

Skip the queues at the airport by using our convenient online check-in service, available 24 to 48 hours before your flight departure.

Airport Check-In:

Arrive at least 2 hours prior to departure for domestic flights and 3 hours prior for international flights.

3. In-Flight Services

Meals & Snacks:

On flights over 2 hours, we offer complimentary meals, snacks, and beverages tailored to your dietary preferences.

In-Flight Entertainment:

Stay entertained with our selection of the latest movies, TV shows, and music. Wi-Fi services are available on most routes.

4. Travel Documentation & Visa Information

Domestic Flights:

All passengers over the age of 18 are required to present a valid government-issued photo ID for domestic flights.

International Flights:

All passengers must have a valid passport with at least 6 months validity beyond their intended date of travel. Visa requirements vary by destination.

5. Health & Safety Guidelines

Your safety is our priority. All Sunshine Airlines aircraft are thoroughly cleaned and sanitized between flights, following strict health protocols.
