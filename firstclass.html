Experience First Class with Sunshine Airlines

What is First Class?

Our First Class is a sanctuary designed for travelers seeking the pinnacle of comfort and service. From the moment you step aboard, you’ll be enveloped in a world of elegance and exclusivity. Enjoy expansive seating with ample legroom, plush bedding, and private suites that offer a tranquil escape from the hustle and bustle of travel. With a dedicated flight crew, your every need is anticipated and catered to, ensuring a seamless journey from start to finish.

How to Book First Class

Booking your First Class seat is a breeze. Simply visit our website or download the Sunshine Airlines mobile app. Select your desired flight, choose the First Class option, and proceed to checkout. For those who prefer personal assistance, our customer service team is ready to help you with your booking. Additionally, you can visit any of our ticketing offices for in-person support. Experience the ease of securing your luxurious journey with just a few clicks.

Who’s Flying First Class?

Our First Class is favored by discerning travelers, including business executives, high-net-worth individuals, and celebrities who appreciate the benefits of privacy, comfort, and superior service. Whether you are traveling for business or pleasure, our First Class cabin is tailored to meet the needs of those who demand the best. Join a community of like-minded travelers who value an extraordinary flight experience and the finer things in life.

Indulge in an array of luxurious amenities designed to enhance your flying experience. Our First Class passengers enjoy gourmet dining curated by renowned chefs, offering a selection of exquisite meals paired with fine wines. Exclusive Benefits

Flying First Class with Sunshine Airlines comes with a host of exclusive benefits. Enjoy priority check-in and boarding, ensuring you spend less time waiting and more time relaxing. Gain access to our luxurious lounges, complete with complimentary refreshments and private areas to unwind before your flight.
