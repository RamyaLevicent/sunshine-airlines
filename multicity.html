How to Book Multicity Flights

Follow these simple steps to book your multicity flights on Sunshine Airlines:

  1. Select the ‘Multicity’ option on the Sunshine Airlines website or app.
  2. Enter your departure city and up to 5 destinations you'd like to visit.
  3. Specify your travel dates for each leg of your journey.
  4. Find the best available flights for each segment of your trip.
  5. Review your itinerary, confirm your details, and finalize your booking.
