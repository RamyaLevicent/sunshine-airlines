How to Book a Flight & Check Your Flight Status

Step 1: Open the App

Download the Sunshine Airlines app from your app store and open it. Log in using your credentials or create a new account if you're a first-time user.

Step 2: Search for a Flight

Enter your departure and destination airports, select your travel dates, and click ‘Search Flights’ to view available options.

Step 3: Select Your Flight

Choose the flight that best suits your schedule. You can view flight details, including available seats, fare types, and more.

Step 4: Complete Booking

Proceed to payment, select add-ons like extra baggage or meals, and confirm your booking. You will receive an e-ticket and confirmation via email and the app.

Step 5: Check Flight Status

To check your flight status, go to the ‘Flight Status’ section, enter your flight number or route, and view real-time updates on delays, gate changes, and more.
