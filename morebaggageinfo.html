Sunshine Airline Baggage Information

Additional Baggage Information

To ensure a smooth travel experience, please review the following guidelines and information regarding your baggage.

Excess Baggage Fees

If your baggage exceeds the allowed weight or size limits, excess baggage fees will apply. Here’s a summary:

  • Economy Class: $100 for each additional bag up to 23 kg (50 lbs).
  • Premium Economy: $100 for each additional bag up to 23 kg (50 lbs).
  • Business Class: $150 for each additional bag up to 32 kg (70 lbs).

Special Considerations for Infants and Children

Sunshine Airline provides special baggage allowances for infants and children:

  • Infants (under 2 years): 1 checked bag up to 10 kg (22 lbs) and a stroller or car seat.
  • Children (2-11 years): Same baggage allowance as Economy Class.

Traveling with Pets

If you plan to travel with your pet, please review our pet policy:

  • Pets can be transported in the cabin or as checked baggage depending on their size and weight.
  • A fee will apply for pet transportation; check our pet policy for more details.
