Online Check-In

Make your journey smoother and faster with Sunshine Airline’s Online Check-In services.

How to Check-In Online

Step 1: Visit Our Website or Mobile App

Start by visiting the Sunshine Airline website or app, and navigate to the Check-In section.

Step 2: Enter Your Details

Enter your booking reference number and last name to access your flight details.

Step 3: Choose Your Seat

Select your preferred seat or make changes before the flight.

Step 4: Add Baggage

Add extra baggage to your booking if needed.

Step 5: Get Your Boarding Pass

Download or print your boarding pass for easy access at the airport.
