First Aid Services

Your Health and Safety at 35,000 Feet

Committed to Your Well-being

At Sunshine Airline, we prioritize your health and safety throughout your journey. Our cabin crew is extensively trained to respond to a wide range of medical conditions, ensuring that you feel safe and cared for from the moment you step onboard. Whether it's addressing minor health concerns or handling an emergency, our team is ready to assist with professionalism and compassion.

State-of-the-Art First Aid Equipment

Every Sunshine Airline flight is equipped with comprehensive medical kits designed to handle various emergencies. Our equipment is maintained and regularly inspected to ensure that it is always ready for use in the event of an emergency. The equipment onboard includes:

  • First Aid Kits for minor injuries (bandages, antiseptics, pain relief)
  • Emergency Medical Kits (EMK) for more serious conditions (medications, medical devices)
  • Oxygen cylinders and masks for passengers experiencing respiratory difficulties
  • Automated External Defibrillators (AEDs) for cardiac emergencies

Our Crew: Trained to Save Lives

Sunshine Airline's cabin crew members undergo extensive first aid and emergency response training to handle a variety of medical situations. Their training includes:

  • CPR (Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation) for adults and children
  • Handling allergic reactions and anaphylaxis
  • Managing respiratory issues using oxygen masks and other equipment
  • Recognizing and addressing heart-related emergencies with defibrillators
  • Communicating effectively with passengers and medical professionals on the ground

Assistance for Special Medical Conditions

If you have a medical condition that requires special attention, we encourage you to inform us in advance so that we can make the necessary arrangements. Passengers with the following conditions can rely on our first aid services and special assistance:

  • Diabetes (assistance with medication and sugar levels)
  • Asthma (oxygen support available onboard)
  • Heart conditions (AEDs are always available for emergencies)
  • Allergic reactions (epinephrine auto-injectors in medical kits)

We also provide support for pregnant passengers and travelers with disabilities. Our staff is trained to offer assistance to ensure a safe and comfortable journey for all.

How We Handle Medical Emergencies

In the event of a medical emergency, our protocol ensures swift and efficient action:

  1. The crew will assess the situation and provide immediate first aid using the onboard medical kits.
  2. If necessary, oxygen masks or AEDs will be used to stabilize the passenger.
  3. The crew will consult with ground-based medical professionals via radio communication for additional guidance.
  4. If the situation requires, the flight may be diverted to the nearest airport for medical assistance on the ground.

We work closely with medical teams on the ground to ensure the passenger receives the best possible care upon arrival.

Preventative Measures for Your Health

Sunshine Airline is committed to helping passengers prevent health issues while traveling. We recommend that you take the following steps to ensure your comfort and safety:

  • Stay hydrated by drinking water frequently during the flight.
  • Move around the cabin or stretch your legs to improve circulation on long-haul flights.
  • If you have a known medical condition, carry any necessary medication with you in your carry-on luggage.
  • Let our crew know if you feel unwell at any point during the flight so they can assist you immediately.

How to Request First Aid Assistance

If you or someone near you is in need of medical assistance during the flight, simply notify one of our cabin crew members. Our crew is trained to discreetly and efficiently respond to medical needs.

You can request assistance by:

  • Pressing the call button located above your seat
  • Speaking directly to a crew member if immediate attention is required

For passengers with pre-existing medical conditions, it is always helpful to inform our staff during check-in or boarding to ensure we are prepared to meet your needs.

Special Medical Requests Before Your Flight

If you have specific medical needs that require additional equipment or care, please contact our customer service team at least 48 hours prior to your flight. We will work with you to accommodate any medical devices, medications, or assistance you may need onboard.

Our goal is to make your flight as comfortable and stress-free as possible, no matter your health requirements.
