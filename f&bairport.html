Sunshine Airlines - Food & Beverage Services

Experience a wide range of delicious meals and beverages during your flight. We offer options to suit every taste in Premium Business, Premium, and Economy classes.

Premium Business Class

In Premium Business Class, you can indulge in a variety of gourmet dishes crafted by our top chefs, along with an excellent selection of wines and beverages.

  • Grilled Chicken Breast with Roasted Vegetables
  • Vegetarian Pasta with Pesto Sauce
  • Pan-seared Salmon with Herb Butter
  • Fresh Fruit Platters and Signature Desserts

Premium Class

In Premium Class, enjoy a mix of healthy and flavorful meal options, curated to satisfy diverse palates with premium beverages.

  • Grilled Paneer Wrap with Mint Chutney
  • Chicken Biryani with Raita
  • Vegetable Stir-Fry with Jasmine Rice
  • Assorted Cheeses and Crackers

Economy Class

Our Economy Class menu offers a selection of tasty and hearty meals, perfect for long or short-haul flights.

  • Chicken Curry with Steamed Rice
  • Vegetarian Lasagna with Side Salad
  • Egg Salad Sandwich with Chips
  • Mini Pancakes with Maple Syrup (for breakfast flights)

How to Select and Order Your Meals

During your booking process, you can choose from the available meal options tailored to your class. Simply select your preference under the ‘Meal Selection’ option while booking or up to 24 hours before departure through ‘Manage Booking’ on our website.

For passengers with specific dietary needs, we offer a variety of special meals, including gluten-free, vegan, and child-friendly options.

Flight Attendant Assistance

Our friendly flight attendants are available to assist you during the meal service. They can help with meal preferences, additional requests, or if you need assistance with beverages.

In Premium and Premium Business classes, enjoy personalized service where attendants will serve your meals course by course, just like in a fine-dining restaurant.
