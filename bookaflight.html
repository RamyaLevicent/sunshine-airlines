How to Book Your Flight

Step 1: Choose Your Destination

Select from our range of international and domestic destinations.

Step 2: Pick Your Travel Dates

Decide your departure and return dates or choose flexible travel options.

Step 3: Enter Passenger Details

Fill in the details of each passenger, including preferences for seats and services.

Step 4: Make a Payment

Pay for your booking with various payment methods available.

Step 5: Select Preferred Cabin Class

Choose Your Cabin Class: Select the comfort level that suits your journey, from economy to premium options.

Step 6: Review and Confirm

Review all details and confirm your booking to receive an instant e-ticket.

Tips for a Smooth Booking Experience

  • Book Early: Secure the best fares and seat selections by booking your flights well in advance.
  • Be Flexible: If your travel dates are flexible, use our flexible date search to find the most economical options.
  • Check Promotions: Keep an eye on our special promotions and discounts to save even more on your bookings.
  • Use Our Mobile App: Manage your bookings on the go with our intuitive mobile app, available for both iOS and Android devices.
  • Join Our Loyalty Program: Sign up for our frequent flyer program to start earning points immediately on your bookings.
