Sunshine Airlines Mobile App

Manage your entire booking experience on the go

With the Sunshine Airlines mobile app, you can easily manage your flight bookings, check real-time updates, and enjoy a smooth travel experience. From searching for flights to receiving real-time updates, our app is designed to make every aspect of your journey easier.

1. Book Flights Anywhere, Anytime

Simple Flight Search: Easily search for and find flights based on your preferred travel dates, destination, and passenger details.

Save Your Preferences: Save frequent traveler details and preferred payment methods to make booking faster and more convenient in the future.

Exclusive Mobile Deals: Get access to app-only discounts and special offers when booking flights directly from your phone.

2. Manage Bookings Effortlessly

Modify Your Reservation: Need to change your travel plans? Use the app to modify your booking, select seats, add extra luggage, or request in-flight services.

Cancel and Refund: Easily cancel or request a refund directly from the app without the hassle of calling customer support.

3. Real-Time Notifications

Stay informed with push notifications for:

  • Flight status updates
  • Gate changes
  • Check-in reminders
  • Flight delays or cancellations

4. Check-In and Get Your Digital Boarding Pass

Skip the airport lines by checking in directly from the app. Download your digital boarding pass, and simply scan your phone at the gate.

5. Loyalty Program Integration

View your Sunshine Loyalty Program points balance, redeem points for flights, and receive loyalty rewards and special promotions right within the app.

6. User-Friendly and Secure

Biometric Login: Log in quickly and securely using face recognition or fingerprint scanning.

Data Protection: We use industry-standard encryption to ensure all your personal and payment information remains safe and secure.

Multiple Languages: The app supports multiple languages, allowing you to switch between them effortlessly.

7. Available on All Devices

Apple iOS: Download the Sunshine Airlines app from the Apple App Store.

Android: Download the app from the Google Play Store.
