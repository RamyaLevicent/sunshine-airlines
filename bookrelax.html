Explore Our Fleet

Airbus A380

The Airbus A380 is the world's largest passenger airliner, offering unmatched luxury, space, and comfort. With a capacity of up to 853 passengers in an all-economy configuration, or 555 passengers in a typical three-class layout, it truly redefines long-haul travel.

Onboard, passengers can enjoy spacious seating, the latest in-flight entertainment systems, and a variety of dining options tailored to their preferences. The aircraft's quiet cabin ensures a more relaxed and peaceful flight experience.

Boeing 787 Dreamliner

The Boeing 787 Dreamliner is known for its innovative design and exceptional fuel efficiency. It offers improved air quality, larger windows, and a quieter cabin, making long-haul flights more comfortable and enjoyable.

With enhanced cabin humidity, passengers feel more refreshed after the flight, minimizing the effects of jet lag. Whether you're flying for business or leisure, the Dreamliner guarantees a smoother and more relaxing journey.

Boeing 737 Max

The Boeing 737 Max is designed for shorter regional flights but offers a modern and comfortable travel experience. Its advanced aerodynamics and fuel-efficient engines provide a quieter and smoother ride.

Passengers can enjoy the new sky interior, complete with LED lighting, improved overhead storage, and ergonomic seating. It's the perfect choice for those traveling on short-haul routes, ensuring a quick yet comfortable trip.
