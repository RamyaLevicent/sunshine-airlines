FAQs

Q1: How can I change my flight booking?

A1: You can easily modify your booking through our Manage Booking section on our website or by contacting our customer support team.

Q2: What is the baggage allowance for my flight?

A2: Our standard baggage allowance includes one checked bag and one carry-on. Additional baggage can be added during booking for a discounted rate.

Q3: Can I earn frequent flyer points on all my bookings?

A3: Yes, all bookings made with Sunshine Airline are eligible for frequent flyer points, which can be redeemed for future flights, upgrades, and more.

Q4: Is travel insurance mandatory?

A4: Travel insurance is optional but highly recommended to protect against unforeseen circumstances like flight cancellations, medical emergencies, and lost baggage.

Q5: How do I select my seat?

A5: During the booking process, you can choose your preferred seat in the Seat Selection section. Alternatively, you can select your seat later through the Manage Booking portal.
