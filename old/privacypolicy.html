Sunshine Airlines Privacy Policy

Effective Date

Sunshine Airlines is committed to protect the Privacy and Security of our customers and website visitors. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you interact with our services, including our website, mobile applications, and other digital platforms.

1. Informations We Collect

We collect personal information directly from you and automatically as you use our services. The types of personal information we may collect include:

  • Identification Information: Name, email address, phone number, postal address, date of birth, gender, and nationality.
  • Payment Information: Credit/debit card details, billing address, and other payment-related data.
  • Travel Information: Flight details, travel preferences, passport number, frequent flyer information, and special requests (e.g., dietary or accessibility needs).
  • Technical Data: IP addresses, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: Information about your use of our website through cookies, pixels, and similar tracking technologies (see our Cookie Policy for more details).

2. How We Use Your Informations

We may use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Booking and Travel Services: To process your flight bookings, manage your reservations, and provide customer service.
  • Communication: To send booking confirmations, travel updates, and respond to inquiries.
  • Personalization: To customize your experience with our special offers, services, and product recommendations.
  • Marketing: With your consent, to send you promotional materials, newsletters, and updates about our services.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations, such as security and customs requirements.
  • Improvement of Services: To analyze how you use our services and improve the user experience.

3. Sharing of Informations

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your data in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist in operating our business, such as payment processors, IT service providers, and marketing agencies.
  • Legal Obligations: We may disclose your information when required by law, to enforce our policies, or to protect our rights, property, or safety.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred to the relevant third party.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration. These include encryption, secure servers, and firewalls. However, no online service is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access and Correction: You can request access to or correction of your personal data at any time.
  • Deletion: You can request the deletion of your personal data, subject to legal and contractual obligations.
  • Marketing Opt-Out: You can opt out of marketing communications by following the instructions in our emails or by contacting us directly.
  • Data Portability: Where applicable, you may have the right to request the transfer of your personal data to another service provider.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and other tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our website and gather data about your browsing activities. For more information, please refer to our [Cookie Policy].

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites or services that are not controlled by us. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third-party sites. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal obligations. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy.
