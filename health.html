Travel Health & COVID Guidelines

General Health Guidelines

Maintaining good health while traveling is essential for an enjoyable and comfortable journey. At Sunshine Airlines, we aim to provide a safe and healthy travel environment for all passengers. Below are some general health tips you should follow during your flight:

Before Your Flight

  • Stay Hydrated: Drink plenty of water before boarding to avoid dehydration, which is common on long flights due to the low humidity inside the cabin.
  • Avoid Heavy Meals: Consuming large or greasy meals right before your flight can lead to indigestion and discomfort during your journey.
  • Medications: If you take regular medication, ensure you carry them in your hand luggage. Always pack extra in case of delays or disruptions.
  • Travel Insurance: It's advisable to have travel insurance that covers health-related incidents abroad, especially if you are traveling to remote locations.
  • Vaccinations: Depending on your destination, you may need vaccinations for diseases like yellow fever, typhoid, or hepatitis. Check with your healthcare provider.

During Your Flight

  • Drink Water: The cabin air is dry, so keep sipping water regularly. Avoid excessive consumption of caffeinated or alcoholic drinks as they can cause dehydration.
  • Move Around: To reduce the risk of deep vein thrombosis (DVT), it's important to move around the cabin periodically. Stand up, stretch, or walk down the aisles when possible.
  • Use Hand Sanitizer: Airports and airplanes can harbor germs, so use hand sanitizer frequently, especially after touching surfaces like seat belts, tray tables, or restroom doors.
  • Take Deep Breaths: On long flights, take time to breathe deeply and relax your muscles. Practice mindfulness or light stretches in your seat to avoid stiffness.
  • Adjust Your Seat: Use lumbar support and adjust your seat frequently to promote circulation and prevent back pain during long-haul flights.

Important: If you feel unwell at any time during the flight, notify our cabin crew immediately. We are equipped to handle basic medical emergencies and will assist you accordingly.

COVID-19 Guidelines

In light of the ongoing global pandemic, Sunshine Airlines has implemented strict health and safety measures to protect all passengers and crew. Please familiarize yourself with our updated COVID-19 guidelines to ensure a safe travel experience for everyone.

Before Traveling

  • Pre-Flight Health Check: If you are feeling unwell or have any symptoms such as fever, cough, or difficulty breathing, we recommend postponing your travel until you are fully recovered.
  • COVID-19 Testing: Depending on your destination, you may need to provide proof of a negative COVID-19 test taken within 48â€“72 hours before departure. Check your destination's regulations in advance.
  • Vaccination Status: Some destinations require proof of COVID-19 vaccination for entry. Ensure that you have a valid vaccination certificate, either digital or paper, as required by your destination.
  • Quarantine Requirements: Familiarize yourself with the quarantine policies of your destination. Some countries may mandate quarantine upon arrival, even with a negative test result.

At the Airport

  • Mask Requirement: Wear a face mask that covers your nose and mouth while inside the airport. Masks are mandatory in all areas, including security checkpoints and boarding gates.
  • Physical Distancing: Maintain at least 6 feet of distance from others, especially in queues and waiting areas. Look out for floor markers and follow the guidance of airport staff.
  • Hand Hygiene: Use the hand sanitizers available throughout the airport, especially after touching shared surfaces or handling luggage.
  • Contactless Check-In: Use our mobile app or self-service kiosks to check in and download your boarding pass, minimizing physical contact with staff and surfaces.

Onboard the Flight

  • Mask-Wearing: Face masks are mandatory throughout the flight, except when eating or drinking. Ensure that your mask covers both your nose and mouth at all times.
  • Seating Arrangements: We strive to leave empty seats between passengers where possible to ensure physical distancing. Please remain seated as much as possible to minimize movement within the cabin.
  • In-Flight Service: Our in-flight service has been modified to reduce contact. Meals and beverages will be provided in pre-packaged containers, and cabin crew will be equipped with protective gear.
  • Cabin Ventilation: Our aircraft are equipped with HEPA filters that remove 99.9% of airborne particles, including viruses and bacteria, ensuring clean air throughout the flight.
