Why Choose Online Boarding Pass?

With Sunshine Airlines, getting your boarding pass online offers convenience and speed. Here's why you should opt for an online boarding pass:

  • Quick and Easy: Skip the lines and avoid the hassle of printing your boarding pass at the airport. Simply download it to your phone or print it at home.
  • 24/7 Availability: Access your boarding pass anytime, anywhere before your flight. No more waiting for kiosks or counters.
  • Environmentally Friendly: Go paperless and contribute to a greener planet by using a digital boarding pass.
  • Flight Updates: Get instant updates about your flight schedule and gate changes directly on your mobile device.
