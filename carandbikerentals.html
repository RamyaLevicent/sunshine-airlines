Sunshine Airlines- Bike & Car Rentals

Terms & Conditions

Booking and Payment

  • Booking Process: Bike rentals can be booked online through the Sunshine Airlines website or app, or at select airport locations. Booking in advance is recommended for availability.
  • Payment Options: Payment can be made using major credit/debit cards, mobile wallets, or online banking.
  • Payment Plans:
    • Full Payment: Pay the full amount at the time of booking.
    • Partial Payment: Pay 50% of the total rental at the time of booking, with the balance due at pick-up.
    • Delayed Payment: Reserve the bike with no upfront cost and pay the full amount upon bike pick-up (available only at selected locations).

Returns & Extensions

  • Return Policy: Bikes should be returned to the designated Sunshine Airlines bike rental location or airport drop-off point by the agreed-upon time. Late returns may incur an additional hourly charge.
  • Extensions: If you wish to extend your rental, please notify us 12 hours before the initial return time. Extensions are subject to availability and additional charges.

Cancellations and Refunds

  • Free Cancellations: Cancellations made 24 hours or more before the pick-up time are eligible for a full refund.
  • Late Cancellations: For cancellations made less than 24 hours before pick-up, a 25% cancellation fee will apply.
  • No-Show Policy: If the bike is not picked up within 2 hours of the scheduled time without prior notification, the reservation will be canceled, and a no-show fee of 50% will apply.

  • Insurance: Optional insurance for theft and damage protection is available.
