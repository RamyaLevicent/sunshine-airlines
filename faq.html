Frequently Asked Questions

1. Flight Booking

Q1: How do I book a flight?
A1: To book a flight, visit our website or app, enter your travel details (departure city, destination, dates), and choose from the available flights. Follow the prompts to complete your booking.

Q2: Can I book a flight for someone else?
A2: Yes, you can book a flight for another person. Just enter their details during the booking process.

Q3: Are there any hidden fees when booking flights?
A3: All fees are disclosed during the booking process. Ensure you review the total cost before confirming your booking.

2. Manage Booking

Q1: How can I manage my booking?
A1: You can manage your booking by logging into your account and navigating to the "Manage Booking" section. Here, you can change flights, add services, or cancel bookings.

Q2: Can I change my flight date after booking?
A2: Yes, you can change your flight date subject to availability and fare differences. There may be fees depending on your ticket type.

Q3: How do I add baggage to my booking?
A3: You can add baggage by accessing your booking in the "Manage Booking" section. Select the option to add baggage and follow the prompts.

3. Special Offers

Q1: How can I find special offers on flights?
A1: Special offers can be found on our website's "Special Offers" page or by subscribing to our newsletter for the latest deals.

Q2: Do I need a promo code to avail of special offers?
A2: Some offers may require a promo code, which will be displayed on the offers page. Ensure you apply it during the booking process.

Q3: Are special offers available for all destinations?
A3: Not all destinations may have special offers. Check the specific offer details for applicable routes.

4. Travel Information

Q1: Where can I find travel information for my destination?
A1: Travel information for various destinations can be found on our website's "Travel Information" section, which includes guidelines, attractions, and tips.

Q2: Do I need a visa to travel to certain destinations?
A2: Visa requirements vary by country. Please check the specific visa requirements for your destination before booking.

Q3: Are there any travel restrictions due to COVID-19?
A3: Travel restrictions may apply based on current health guidelines. Check the destination's official government website for the latest updates.

5. Cancellation of Tickets

Q1: How can I cancel my ticket?
A1: To cancel your ticket, log into your account, go to "Manage Booking," and select "Cancel Flight." Follow the prompts to complete the cancellation.

Q2: Is there a cancellation fee?
A2: Yes, cancellation fees may apply based on the fare rules of your ticket. You will be informed of any applicable fees before you confirm.

Q3: How long will it take to receive a refund?
A3: Refund processing times can vary. Typically, it takes 5-10 business days for the refund to reflect in your account after the cancellation is processed.

6. Flight Cancellation

Q1: What happens if my flight is canceled by the airline?
A1: If your flight is canceled, you will be notified via email or SMS. You can rebook a new flight or request a full refund.

Q2: Am I entitled to compensation for a canceled flight?
A2: Compensation eligibility depends on the airline’s policy and local regulations. Check with customer support for specific details.

Q3: How can I rebook my canceled flight?
A3: You can rebook your canceled flight through the "Manage Booking" section on our website or app.

7. Flight Delay

Q1: What should I do if my flight is delayed?
A1: If your flight is delayed, check your email or the app for updates. You can also find the latest status on the airline's website.

Q2: Will I receive compensation for a delayed flight?
A2: Compensation depends on the length of the delay and the airline's policies. Please review their compensation policy for details.

Q3: How do I find out the status of my delayed flight?
A3: Check the flight status on our website or app for real-time updates on your delayed flight.

8. Travel Insurance

Q1: Do you offer travel insurance?
A1: Yes, travel insurance options are available during the booking process. Select the coverage you need to protect against unforeseen events.

Q2: How can I file a claim for travel insurance?
A2: To file a claim, contact the insurance provider directly and provide the necessary documentation. Refer to the insurance policy for specific instructions.

Q3: Is travel insurance mandatory?
A3: Travel insurance is not mandatory, but it is highly recommended for added protection during your travels.
