Short-Term Parking

Ideal for quick visits to the airport, short-term parking is located near the terminal. With easy access and affordable rates, it's perfect for drop-offs and pickups. Enjoy secure parking and a stress-free experience. Payment is made easy with our digital kiosks or app.

Benefits of Choosing Short-Term Parking

Close Proximity to the Terminal

Our short-term parking lot is located just a few steps away from the terminal entrance. You can easily transport your luggage without the hassle of long walks or waiting for shuttle services.

Flexible and Affordable Rates

Short-term parking rates are designed to be budget-friendly for brief visits. You pay only for the time you need, with competitive hourly rates. Ideal for those quick trips, you can park for just a few minutes or several hours without overspending.

Easy Payment Options

We offer various payment methods, including cash, credit/debit cards, and mobile payment options. This flexibility ensures a smooth experience as you enter and exit the parking facility.

Safety and Security

Our short-term parking facility is equipped with 24/7 surveillance and regular security patrols, ensuring the safety of your vehicle while you are away. Rest assured that your car is protected in a secure environment.

Ideal for Visitors and Guests

Short-term parking is perfect not only for travelers but also for airport visitors and those picking up friends and family. Enjoy the convenience of parking close to the terminal without worrying about long-term fees.

Accessibility Features

The short-term parking lot includes designated accessible spaces for those with mobility challenges. These spaces are conveniently located near the terminal for easy access.

No Reservations Needed

Unlike long-term parking, short-term parking doesn’t require reservations. Just arrive, park your vehicle, and head straight to the terminal—perfect for spontaneous trips or last-minute airport visits.
