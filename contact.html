Frequently Asked Questions

What is your baggage policy?
Our baggage policy allows one carry-on bag and one personal item per passenger. For detailed information on checked baggage allowances and restrictions, please refer to our baggage policy page.
How can I change my flight booking?
You can change your booking online through the "Manage Booking" option on our website or contact our customer service for assistance.
What are the check-in procedures?
Check-in can be done online starting 24 hours before departure or at the airport. Make sure to arrive at least two hours before domestic flights and three hours before international flights.
