Baggage Information

Your complete guide to baggage allowances, fees, and online check-in at Sunshine Airline.

Carry-On Baggage Allowance

Each passenger is allowed:

  • 1 Carry-On Bag: Maximum dimensions 55 cm x 35 cm x 25 cm, up to 7 kg (15 lbs).
  • 1 Personal Item: Handbag, laptop bag, or small backpack fitting under the seat.

Checked Baggage Allowance

Baggage allowances vary depending on ticket type and destination:

  • Economy Class: 1 checked bag up to 23 kg (50 lbs).
  • Premium Economy: 2 checked bags, each up to 23 kg (50 lbs).
  • Business Class: 2 checked bags, each up to 32 kg (70 lbs).

Prohibited and Restricted Items

For your safety, the following items are prohibited in carry-on and checked baggage:

  • Explosives and flammable substances (e.g., fireworks, gas canisters).
  • Firearms and weapons without proper authorization.
  • Sharp objects like knives and scissors.
  • Hazardous materials (e.g., poisonous chemicals, corrosives).
  • Loose lithium batteries not installed in a device.

Special Items and Sports Equipment

Sunshine Airline allows the transport of special items such as:

  • Golf clubs, bicycles, and surfboards (fees may apply for oversized items).
  • Musical instruments, either checked or carried on, subject to space limitations.
