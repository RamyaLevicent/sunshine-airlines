Important Places to Visit in Paris

  • Eiffel Tower: Iconic symbol of Paris offering stunning views from its observation decks.
  • Louvre Museum: Home to thousands of works of art, including the Mona Lisa and the Venus de Milo.
  • Notre-Dame Cathedral: A masterpiece of French Gothic architecture, renowned for its intricate façade and stunning stained glass.
  • Montmartre and Sacré-Cœur Basilica: A historic district known for its bohemian charm and the breathtaking basilica at its summit.
  • Champs-Élysées and Arc de Triomphe: A famous avenue lined with shops and cafés leading to the monumental arch.
  • Palace of Versailles: A short trip from Paris, this opulent palace and its gardens are a UNESCO World Heritage site.
  • Seine River Cruise: Enjoy picturesque views of Paris’s landmarks from the water, especially beautiful at sunset.
