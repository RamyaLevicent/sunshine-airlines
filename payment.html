Cancellation and Refund Procedure

Step 1: To cancel a booking or payment, go to your account section and find the "Cancel Booking" or "Refund" option.

Step 2: Click on the "Cancel" button and confirm your cancellation request. Make sure to check the cancellation policy.

Step 3: If applicable, request a refund by filling out the refund form, providing details like the booking reference and payment method.

Step 4: Once submitted, your refund request will be processed within 3-5 business days. You will receive an email confirmation.

Step 5: If eligible, the refund amount will be credited back to your original payment method within the specified time frame.
