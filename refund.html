Sunshine Airlines - FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions - Refund Requests

How do I request a refund for my booking?
You can request a refund by filling out the refund request form on our website. Ensure you provide all necessary details such as your booking reference, reason for the refund, and any additional documentation required.
How long does it take to process a refund?
Refund processing times depend on the payment method used. For credit card payments, it may take 5-7 business days. For other payment methods, it could take up to 10 business days.
Can I get a refund if I cancel my flight due to illness?
Yes, in cases of illness, refunds can be requested. You will need to provide medical documentation to support your claim.
What happens if the airline cancels my flight?
If the airline cancels your flight, you are entitled to a full refund or the option to reschedule your flight at no additional cost.
Can I get a refund for non-refundable tickets?
In some cases, refunds for non-refundable tickets may be issued if the cancellation is due to an emergency or a significant schedule change by the airline.
